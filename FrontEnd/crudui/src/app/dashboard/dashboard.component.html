Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI), Mumbai

Established in 1887 as the Victoria Jubilee Technical Institute, VJTI has been a trailblazer in India's engineering education, research, and training landscape. VJTI has played a crucial role in fostering industrial development across India and has contributed to the establishment of prestigious institutions like the IITs and RECs.

Vision

To establish global leadership in the field of Technology and develop competent human resources for providing service to society.

Mission

To provide students with comprehensive knowledge of engineering principles, foster creativity, and contribute to National and International development.
